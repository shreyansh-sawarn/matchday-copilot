## Algorithm

1. Select highest priority task.
2. Implement incrementally.
3. Validate functionality.
4. Update runtime.
5. Repeat until objectives are met.

---

## Success Criteria

---

## Failure Conditions

Critical blocker → Escalate.

Sprint objective unattainable → Re-prioritize.
