# Demo Engine

## Metadata

- Version: 1.0.0
- Layer: Execution
- Owner: Demo Engine
- Authority: High
- Status: Stable

---

## Purpose

Transform the completed solution into a persuasive, reliable and memorable demonstration.

---

## Objective

Maximize judge understanding and perceived value within the available presentation time.

---

## Reasoning Stage

Communicate

---

## Decision Owned

Presentation Decision

---

## Consumes

* Working Software
* Review Report
* Score Estimate
* Runtime

---

## Produces

* Demo Plan
* Demo Script
* Presentation Flow
* Speaking Points

---

## Algorithm

1. Identify key value proposition.
2. Select demonstration workflow.
3. Remove unnecessary detail.
4. Optimize presentation flow.
5. Produce final demo plan.

---

## Success Criteria

Judges understand the solution quickly and clearly.

---

## Failure Conditions

Working software unavailable → Use fallback demo.

Demo exceeds time limit → Compress flow.

---

## Validation

Verify clarity, timing and demonstration reliability.
